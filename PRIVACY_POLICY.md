# SkyChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 9, 2026

## What data we collect

SkyChat collects your email address when you create an account. This is used solely for authentication and account recovery.

## What we don't do

- We do not use analytics or tracking tools.
- We do not share your data with third parties.
- We do not sell your personal information.

## Data storage

Your email and chat messages are stored on our servers to provide the service. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, reach out at https://github.com/skychatorg/skychat/issues.
